# Special Generation Scenarios

## Scenario 1: User Provides Technical Constraints

**USER INPUT:** "Portrait, but must be shot at 50mm"

**APPROACH:** Fix the constrained element, vary other dimensions dramatically. Examples:

**Option 1 - Vary composition & angle**:
- Variation A: Low angle with leading lines creating power
- Variation B: Eye level centered symmetric
- Variation C: High angle with negative space for vulnerability

**Option 2 - Vary lighting**:
- Variation A: Hard side light, dramatic mood
- Variation B: Soft window light, gentle mood
- Variation C: Golden hour back light, romantic mood

**Option 3 - Vary depth & atmosphere**:
- Variation A: Shallow DoF (f/1.8) with soft haze
- Variation B: Balanced DoF (f/4) crisp and clear
- Variation C: Deep DoF (f/11) with rain atmosphere

**Or combine**: vary lighting + composition, mood + camera angle, color + atmosphere, etc.

Choose what creates the most meaningful differences for the specific concept.

## Scenario 2: User Provides Vague Input

**USER INPUT:** "Something cool with a person"

**APPROACH:** Ask clarifying questions OR provide three dramatically different interpretations:
- Variation A: Urban street environmental portrait
- Variation B: Studio fashion close-up
- Variation C: Natural outdoor adventure shot

## Scenario 3: User Requests Specific Mood Only

**USER INPUT:** "Melancholic feeling, any subject"

**APPROACH:** Explore different subjects that embody the mood with varied technical approaches:
- Variation A: Portrait in rain, cool tones, soft light
- Variation B: Empty urban scene, blue hour, long exposure
- Variation C: Interior scene, single figure, window light

## Scenario 4: User Provides Reference Image

**USER INPUT:** "Like this image but with my subject" + reference

**APPROACH:** Extract technical DNA from reference, apply to user's subject with variations:
- Variation A: Exact recreation of reference approach
- Variation B: Same mood but different technical execution
- Variation C: Alternative interpretation of the reference feel
