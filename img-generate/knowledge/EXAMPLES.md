# Additional Generation Examples

## Example: Product Request

**USER INPUT:** "Luxury watch on a table, premium feel, clean look"

**CONCEPT:** Product hero shot emphasizing craftsmanship and premium positioning

**VARIATION A: High-Key Studio Precision**
```
Luxury watch on marble surface shot from 45-degree high angle on 100mm macro lens,
high-key lighting with soft overhead softbox at 5500K neutral and fill cards
eliminating shadows, balanced depth of field keeping watch face and case sharp
with gentle background fade, minimalist composition with watch on right third
and ample negative space, clean and technical atmosphere with subtle reflections
on polished surfaces
```
*Technical approach:* Clean studio control, high-key, even illumination, catalogue precision

**VARIATION B: Dramatic Side Light Character**
```
Luxury watch on dark wood surface shot from eye-level on 85mm lens, hard side
light from single source at 4500K warm creating sculptural shadows and specular
highlights on metal, shallow depth of field with watch face sharp and background
dissolving to black, asymmetric composition with watch emerging from shadow,
sophisticated and dramatic atmosphere emphasizing craftsmanship through light
and shadow interplay
```
*Technical approach:* Low-key drama, hard sculptural light, chiaroscuro contrast

**VARIATION C: Natural Light Lifestyle Context**
```
Luxury watch on wrist resting on leather armchair arm shot from 45-degree angle
on 50mm lens, soft window light from left at 5000K neutral creating gentle
modelling, balanced depth of field with watch sharp and room environmental
context softly visible, naturalistic composition suggesting lifestyle rather than
product isolation, warm and aspirational atmosphere with practical light from
table lamp adding depth
```
*Technical approach:* Contextual lifestyle, natural window light, environmental storytelling

**RECOMMENDED START:** Variation A — High-key studio approach is safest for premium product work and easiest to execute consistently

## Example: Architectural Request

**USER INPUT:** "Modern building exterior, impressive and bold"

**CONCEPT:** Architectural hero emphasizing scale, form, and contemporary design

**VARIATION A: Wide Angle Drama**
```
Modern glass and steel building shot from ground level on 24mm wide angle lens
looking up at dramatic low angle, blue hour lighting at 6500K cool with warm
interior lights glowing through windows, deep depth of field keeping entire
facade sharp from foreground to sky, asymmetric composition with building
dominating right two-thirds and sky on left, bold and monumental atmosphere
with clear twilight revealing crisp architectural lines
```
*Technical approach:* Wide angle distortion for monumentality, blue hour colour contrast

**VARIATION B: Compressed Graphic Form**
```
Modern building facade shot from distance on 135mm telephoto at eye-level angle,
harsh noon sun at 5500K neutral creating high contrast geometric shadows, deep
depth of field rendering entire facade sharp with compressed layered depth,
centered symmetric composition emphasizing architectural pattern and repetition,
graphic and abstract atmosphere treating building as sculptural form with stark
shadow play
```
*Technical approach:* Telephoto compression, harsh noon light, graphic abstraction

**VARIATION C: Golden Hour Warmth**
```
Modern building shot from 45-degree angle on 35mm lens during golden hour, warm
3500K side lighting sculpting architectural form with long dramatic shadows,
balanced depth of field keeping building sharp with environmental context visible,
rule of thirds composition with building on right and golden sky on left, warm
and inviting atmosphere humanizing modern design through flattering natural light
```
*Technical approach:* Moderate wide angle, golden hour warmth, humanized approach

**RECOMMENDED START:** Variation A — Wide angle low perspective is most effective for creating "impressive and bold" feeling
